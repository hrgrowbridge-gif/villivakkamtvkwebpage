Why Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam in Villivakkam?

For too long, promises have stayed on paper. Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam brings real accountability, direct delivery, and a people-first council to solve the issues that matter most.

  • Free bus travel for women and households
  • Drug-free schools and strong local healthcare
  • Real parks, safe roads, and clean public spaces
  • Direct response to every complaint and issue
